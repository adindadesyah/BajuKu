About Us

BajuKu is a store that sells children's clothing of various ages and sizes. BajuKu will stock a wide selection of clothing, including shirts, pants, dresses and outerwear, both in new and used condition, as well as a variety of styles and designs to appeal to different tastes. BajuKu will appeal to parents and caregivers looking for affordable and durable clothing options for their children.

    Our advantage:

  • BajuKu to Kids
    We use environmentally friendly materials and production methods, to ensure long-lasting and high-quality products. These qualities ensure our products have a long life cycle and can be reused more than once. Our trade-in model allows you to share your oversized clothes with other families, nothing goes to waste.
  • BajuKu to Planet
    BajuKu children's clothing is designed with the needs of your little one in mind. We pay special attention to ensuring comfort and we only use high-quality materials that won't irritate your child's sensitive skin.
